1. How We Use AI

homeAImatch uses Claude (developed by Anthropic) to:

  • Match properties to buyers: Your lifestyle quiz answers are analysed by AI to score how well each property in our database matches your preferences
  • Generate buyer personas: A summary profile is created from your quiz answers to help agents understand your needs
  • Provide match reasoning: The AI explains why specific properties were recommended to you

2. Limitations

AI-generated content is indicative and may contain inaccuracies. Specifically:

  • Match scores are estimates based on available data, not guarantees of suitability
  • Buyer personas are AI interpretations and may not perfectly reflect your preferences
  • The AI does not have access to information not provided in the quiz or property listing
  • The AI does not provide financial, legal, or professional property advice

3. Data Handling

When processing your quiz answers, we send them to Anthropic's API. Anthropic does not use API data to train their models. Your data is processed in transit and not stored by Anthropic beyond the duration of the API request.

4. Human Oversight

No fully automated decisions with legal or similarly significant effects are made about you. AI assists in matching and scoring, but all property decisions are made by you and the agents you interact with.

5. Your Rights

Under GDPR Article 22, you have the right not to be subject to decisions based solely on automated processing. If you believe an AI-generated output has affected you unfairly, contact us at hello@homeaimatch.com and we will review the matter manually.
